How it works

Simple enough that
everyone actually uses it.

Three layers, each one valuable on its own.

1

Build Luna's profile

Add your dog's vet records, routines, medications, and care notes. Everything in one place — shareable as a full profile or a custom snapshot depending on who needs it.

2

Post a care need

Need someone to walk her Tuesday at 3pm? Post it. Your circle gets a simple link — no app download, no account. They tap to claim, see exactly what to do, and check off when they're done.

3

Everyone stays in the loop

You see updates as they happen — task check-offs, photo updates, anything out of the ordinary. Your dog is cared for. You didn't have to coordinate it all yourself.
